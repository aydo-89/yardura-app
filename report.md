# Yardura Landing Page Update: Research & Recommendations (July 19 2025)

## Background
Yardura is repositioning from a single‐product, pet‑waste storage solution (WasteWell) to a broader *premium outdoor‑living brand* that will offer **pet and yard solutions**.  The current landing page (which focuses on WasteWell) needs to be redesigned to reflect this expanded vision.  This report summarizes design research and provides recommendations and assets for the update.

## Design recommendations for Yardura

### 1. Overall positioning
* **Headline & tagline** – Immediately communicate Yardura’s expanded mission, e.g. **“Outdoor pet and yard products”** with a subheading like **“Premium solutions for outdoor living with pets.”** This clarifies that the brand offers more than a single pet‑waste product.

* **Intro paragraph** – Briefly describe Yardura’s mission: designing high‑quality outdoor products that harmonize pet care with yard aesthetics.  Mention the first product (WasteWell) and hint at upcoming yard‑storage and organization solutions.

### 2. Hero section
* Use an immersive hero area that fills most of the initial viewport.  Ideally this would feature a high‑quality photograph or short video of a beautifully maintained backyard with a pet enjoying the space; however, while prototyping you can use abstract background patterns to evoke nature and premium quality.  A concise headline and subheading should overlay the image, accompanied by a **wait‑list sign‑up form** (email field and CTA button).  This replicates the effective practice of overlaying a form on the hero image【107825389592422†L150-L156】 and keeps key elements above the fold【107825389592422†L224-L233】.

* For the CTA button, use an accent colour (e.g., dark green or charcoal) that contrasts with the background.  Offer value in the copy (e.g., “Join the waitlist for early access & special pricing”).

### 3. Sections and content

| Section | Purpose & content |
| --- | --- |
| **Product spotlight: WasteWell** | Introduce WasteWell as the launch product with a high‑quality photo (render or prototype).  Describe its benefits: hygienic pet‑waste disposal, odor‑control, durable materials.  Reinforce that it’s the first of several solutions.  Include a small CTA (e.g., “Learn more” or “Join waitlist”). |
| **Future yard solutions** | Outline upcoming products: modular outdoor storage bins, composting solutions, decorative planters or integrated yard organizers.  Use conceptual illustrations or icons to hint at the product categories.  Highlight features such as weather‑resistant materials, modern aesthetics and pet‑friendly design. |
| **Why Yardura?** | Communicate brand values: premium quality, sustainable materials, thoughtful design that enhances outdoor living with pets.  Use icons to illustrate benefits (e.g., durability, eco‑friendliness, elegant design). |
| **Testimonials / Social proof** | Once available, showcase testimonials or early adopter quotes.  Initially, consider adding a “Coming soon” teaser or invite visitors to share their experiences.  Trust‑building elements are essential【107825389592422†L258-L261】. |
| **Newsletter & wait‑list** | Repeat the sign‑up invitation at the bottom of the page with a clear CTA.  Explain what subscribers get (updates, launch offers, design insights). |

### 4. Visual style
* **Colour palette** – Use natural tones: greens and teals (to suggest gardens), warm earth browns and beige, contrasted with charcoal or dark green for CTAs.  This palette evokes outdoor living while remaining sophisticated.

* **Imagery** – Use high‑resolution photographs of backyards, patios, pets enjoying outdoor spaces and modern yard‑storage solutions.  While prototyping, you can use abstract backgrounds that suggest nature.  Avoid generic stock photos; instead choose images with a premium feel.  Use overlapping images to showcase multiple products or lifestyle scenes【107825389592422†L166-L178】.

* **Typography** – Select clean, modern fonts with good readability.  Use larger sizes for headlines and comfortable line spacing for body text.

* **Layout** – Maintain generous white space.  Keep navigation simple (e.g., Home, Products, About, Wait‑list).  Ensure the design is **mobile responsive**【374905164339332†L342-L344】【107825389592422†L262-L266】 with breakpoints for different screen sizes.

### 5. Conversion & lead capture
* **Prominent CTAs** – Follow the winning formula by placing clear CTAs at key points: in the hero section and near the bottom【107825389592422†L241-L244】.  Make them action‑oriented (“Join the waitlist”, “Get early access”).

* **Customer‑focused messaging** – Emphasise the benefits of Yardura products for users: cleaner yards, more enjoyable outdoor time with pets, stylish storage solutions.  Headlines should address customer needs and highlight differentiators【107825389592422†L246-L252】.

* **Social proof & trust** – As products launch, include testimonials, customer logos or media mentions to build credibility【107825389592422†L258-L261】.

* **SEO & accessibility** – Write descriptive alt text for images, use semantic HTML tags, and include relevant keywords (e.g., “premium pet‑waste container”, “outdoor living with pets”).
